<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Battle Systems">

At the heart of the **bleach mirrors high genre** is a battle system that blends speed with card-driven strategy. Players form a team of three characters and engage in real-time combat where movement and skill execution are handled through a deck of Action Cards. Each card represents a specific technique, ranging from standard strikes to powerful Kidō spells.

| Mechanic | Description | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Action Cards** | Skill cards that appear at the bottom of the screen | Enables strategic skill sequencing |
| **Mortal Blows** | Cinematic ultimate abilities with high damage | Game-changing finishers |
| **Soul Drive** | Coordinated attacks involving all team members | High burst damage and combo extension |
| **Cover Change** | Switching characters during an enemy combo | Mitigates damage and resets neutral |
| **Counter Mechanics** | Precisely timed blocks or dodges | Rewards high-skill reactive play |

The tactical layer is further enhanced by the "Target Break" system, where focusing attacks on specific enemy weaknesses can temporarily stun them, opening a window for a full-team Soul Drive.

<Callout type="warning" title="Energy Management">
Using Action Cards consumes a regenerating energy bar. Spamming high-cost cards will leave you defenseless against counters, so balancing low-cost pokes with high-cost finishers is essential.
</Callout>
