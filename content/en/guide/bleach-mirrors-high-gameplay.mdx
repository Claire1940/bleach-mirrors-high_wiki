The heart of **Bleach Mirrors High gameplay** lies in its "Immersive 13 Court Guard Squads Action" system. <Section icon={Zap} title="Advanced Combat Strategies">

Once you have mastered the basics of **Bleach Mirrors High gameplay**, you can begin utilizing advanced techniques to dominate the leaderboards. One such technique is "Card Cancelling," where you use a high-speed Action Card to interrupt the animation of a slower, heavier attack, allowing for seamless combos.

Another critical strategy involves the "Soul Drive" timing. If you trigger a Soul Drive exactly when an enemy is in a "Staggered" state, the damage multiplier increases by 50%. This is essential for taking down the high-health bosses found in the 2026 endgame raids.

| Strategy | Execution | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Perfect Parry** | Time a defensive card at the moment of impact | Refills 25% of your Action Gauge |
| **Elemental Chain** | Use Ice then Lightning cards in sequence | Triggers a 'Superconduct' stun effect |
| **Switch-In Attack** | Switch characters during a Mortal Blow | Maintains combo meter and increases pressure |
