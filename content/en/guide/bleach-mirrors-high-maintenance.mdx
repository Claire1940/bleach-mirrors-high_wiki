<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat System and Chain Swap Mechanics">

Combat is a dynamic blend of tactical planning and fast-paced execution. Players build a team of three characters and engage in battles where timing is everything. Instead of traditional turn-based menus, combat utilizes an Action Card system. These cards represent various attacks, including physical strikes, defensive maneuvers, and powerful Kido or Hado spells.

The active timer adds a layer of real-time pressure, forcing players to make quick decisions as the action unfolds. Managing this timer while monitoring your card options is the key to maintaining offensive pressure. The system encourages players to chain attacks together, building up momentum to trigger powerful team-based maneuvers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Active Timer">
    Engage in basic combat to generate action points and fill your tactical timer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Play Action Cards">
    Select Kido, Hado, or physical cards to execute precise tactical maneuvers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Combo Swap">
    Chain your attacks and swap in a bench character to maintain offensive momentum.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash Mortal Blow">
    Activate your character's ultimate ability to deliver a cinematic, high-damage finishing strike.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Successfully executing these steps allows players to perform "Soul Drive" attacks, where all three team members coordinate to unleash massive damage. Additionally, mastering the "Counter Hit" mechanic is crucial for high-level play. Timing an attack precisely as an enemy strikes will interrupt their action, leaving them vulnerable to a devastating counter-offensive.

<Callout type="tip" title="Counter Timing">
Watch the enemy's visual cues closely. Triggering a Counter Hit not only stops their momentum but also briefly freezes their active timer, giving you a massive tactical advantage.
