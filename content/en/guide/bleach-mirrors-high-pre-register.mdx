<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat System: Action Cards & Soul Drive">

The gameplay in **Bleach Mirrors High** is described as "Stylish BLEACH battles" that blend speed with strategy. Unlike traditional hack-and-slash games, this title utilizes a team-based mechanic where your choices of **Action Cards** dictate the flow of combat. You build a team of three and cycle through their abilities in real-time.

| Mechanic | Description | Strategic Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Action Cards** | Skill-based cards used for attacks | Prevents button mashing, rewards timing |
| **Mortal Blows** | Explosively animated finishers | High damage, clears waves of Hollows |
| **Soul Drive** | Coordinated team attacks | Maximizes synergy between 3 units |
| **Zanpakuto Custom** | Altering weapon style/appearance | Tailors gameplay to your preference |

The "Soul Drive" system is particularly noteworthy, as it allows for "dream team" combinations. For example, pairing Ichigo with Renji might trigger a specific Soul Drive animation and effect that differs from pairing him with a new character like Shirin.

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategy Tip">
Focus on collecting Action Cards that have "Chain" properties. These allow you to link attacks between your three team members without depleting your energy bar too quickly.
</Callout>
