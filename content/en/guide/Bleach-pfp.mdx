Creating a high-quality icon requires more than just a screenshot. Follow these steps to ensure your profile looks professional and sharp.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Source Material">
    Choose between the manga art (for a classic, inked look) or the anime (for vibrant colors). The TYBW anime is recommended for 2026 due to its superior lighting and line work.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the 'Aura' Moment">
    Look for scenes where the character is using a Bankai or a signature move. The "Blade is Me" episode for Ichigo or the "Senjumaru Bankai" reveal are goldmines for high-quality frames.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Crop for the Circle">
    Most platforms use circular crops. Ensure the character's eyes are positioned in the upper third of the circle to maintain focus and balance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Adjust Contrast and Saturation">
    Slightly increase the contrast to make the black Shihakusho (Soul Reaper robes) stand out against the background effects like spiritual pressure (Reiatsu).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Low Quality">
Avoid using screenshots from the original 2004 anime run unless they have been professionally remastered. The lower resolution and 4:3 aspect ratio often result in blurry icons on modern high-DPI smartphone screens.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Visual Comparison: Manga vs. Anime Styles">

When choosing your **Bleach pfp**, you must decide between the stark, detailed ink-work of Tite Kubo's manga or the fluid, colorful animation of the series. Both have distinct advantages depending on the platform you are using.

Manga-style icons are often seen as more "sophisticated" or "hardcore." The heavy use of black ink and negative space creates a bold look that is very readable even at small sizes. On the other hand, the anime—especially the Thousand-Year Blood War—offers incredible particle effects and glowing Reiatsu that can make a profile feel more alive and energetic.

| Feature | Manga Icon | Anime (TYBW) Icon |
|---------|------------|-------------------|
| Readability | Excellent (High Contrast) | Good (Busy Backgrounds) |
| Detail | Sharp Linework | Advanced Shading |
| Color | Black and White | Vibrant/Neon Effects |
| Vibe | Classic/Artistic | Modern/Cinematic |
