To maximize your combat efficiency, you must master the relationship between different card types and their respective battlefield outcomes.

| Card Type | Primary Effect | Optimal Usage Scenario |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Strike (Red)** | High physical damage, interrupt enemy attacks | When the enemy is preparing a slow, heavy strike |
| **Kido (Blue)** | Ranged elemental damage, applies status ailments | To chip away at flying enemies or group targets |
| **Guard (Green)** | Reduces incoming damage, builds counter gauge | When facing unavoidable boss ultimate attacks |
| **Support (Yellow)** | Heals active character, boosts attack speed | Use during down-times between enemy attack phases |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Customization Guide">

One of the standout features is the ability to customize your Soul Reaper. This allows you to tailor both your aesthetic appearance and your mechanical playstyle to suit your preferences. Follow these steps to optimize your avatar setup.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Define Your Visual Identity">
    Access the customization menu from the home screen to alter Shirin's facial features, hair style, and starting Soul Reaper robes. These choices are purely cosmetic and can be changed at any time without consuming resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Zanpakuto Form">
    Choose the baseline shape and sealed form of your Zanpakuto. This choice influences your basic attack range and swing speed, laying the foundation for your overall combat rhythm.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Imprint Fighting Styles">
    Equip specific style memories unlocked through the main story. These memories alter your heavy attacks and determine whether your avatar plays as a nimble dual-wielder, a heavy bruiser, or a swift ranged fighter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Map Action Card Loadouts">
    Customize the composition of your active combat deck. Select which elemental and utility cards will spawn during battles to ensure they align with your team's overall synergy.
  </Step>
</Steps>
