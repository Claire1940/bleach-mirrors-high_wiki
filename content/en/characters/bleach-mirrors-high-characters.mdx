<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Deep Character Customization & Soul Reaper Creation">

One of the most innovative features involving **Bleach Mirrors High characters** is the ability to create a custom protagonist. Players can choose between a male or female character (Shirin or Shurin) and modify their appearance extensively. The gameplay for **Bleach Mirrors High characters** revolves around a strategic, card-driven combat system. Characters are assigned specific attributes (Light/Dark system) and rarities that determine their power ceiling.

Combat is fast-paced but requires careful resource management. Players must balance their "dodge gauge" (Flash Step) while cycling through skill cards to build up their level for a devastating Soul Drive finisher.

| Combat Mechanic | Description | Strategic Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chain Attack** | Switch characters mid-combo | Reduces swap cooldown for the exiting unit |
| **Soul Drive** | Ultimate cinematic finisher | Deals massive damage and resets level |
| **Dodge Gauge** | Limited Flash Step resource | Essential for avoiding high-damage skills |
| **Attribute Advantage** | Light vs Dark elemental system | Increases damage dealt by up to 50% |

<Callout type="warning" title="Cooldown Management">
When using a Chain Attack, the character being swapped out receives a reduced cooldown (e.g., 7 seconds instead of 10). Always use Chain Attacks when your current character's dodge gauge is empty to maintain offensive pressure.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Master Chain Attacks">

Mastering the interaction between your **Bleach Mirrors High characters** is the key to winning high-level PvP and PvE encounters. The Chain Attack system allows for seamless transitions that keep the enemy staggered while managing your team's stamina.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Combo Meter">
    Initiate combat with basic attack cards to fill the level gauge. Watch for the enemy's dodge gauge to deplete.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trigger the Skill Card">
    Activate a character-specific skill (like Rukia's Sode no Shirayuki). This creates an opening for a follow-up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Chain Switch">
    While the skill animation is active, tap the portrait of your next character. They will enter the field with a strike.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Cooldowns">
    The previously used character will now have a shortened cooldown, allowing them to return to the fight faster than a standard swap.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
