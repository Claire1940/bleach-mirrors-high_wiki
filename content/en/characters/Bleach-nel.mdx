<Section icon={Target} title="Who is Nelliel Tu Odelschwanck?">

When exploring the vast universe of Tite Kubo's legendary manga and anime, **Bleach nel** stands out as one of the most compelling and unique characters. Originally introduced as a quirky, green-haired child Hollow in the endless deserts of Hueco Mundo, her true identity is far more prestigious. Nelliel Tu Odelschwanck is the former 3rd Espada of Sosuke Aizen's Arrancar army, possessing power that rivals the strongest entities in the series. Unlike her bloodthirsty peers, Nelliel possesses a deeply ingrained sense of honor and a pacifistic philosophy that sets her apart from other Hollows.

Her fall from the ranks of the Espada was not due to a lack of strength, but rather a victim of betrayal. The envious Nnoitra Gilga, who was then the 8th Espada, despised Nelliel for her superior power and her refusal to engage in meaningless combat. Partnering with the cunning Szayelaporro Granz, Nnoitra engineered a sneak attack, cracking Nelliel's Hollow mask and casting her out of Las Noches. This traumatic damage caused her spiritual pressure to leak, reverting her into an amnesiac child form.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Dual Forms & Personality Shift">

The character of **Bleach nel** is defined by her split existence. In her child form, known simply as Nel Tu, she is cheerful, highly energetic, and speaks with a prominent lisp. She forms an immediate, deep emotional attachment to Ichigo Kurosaki, whom she affectionately calls "Itsygo." Despite her childish demeanor, she retains a pure heart, often trying to protect her friends despite her lack of offensive capabilities. In stark contrast, her adult form is a mature, intellectual, and highly capable warrior who maintains her core values of empathy and honor.

When she reverts to her adult form, her personality shifts back to that of a seasoned commander. She is composed, analytical, and highly protective of those she cares about. She refuses to fight opponents who are injured or weak, viewing such actions as beastly rather than honorable. This duality makes her one of the most psychologically complex Arrancar in the series.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Zanpakuto and Signature Abilities">

When forced into combat, Nelliel wields a Zanpakuto named Gamuza. Unlike the Soul Reapers who release their swords to perform Shikai and Bankai, Arrancar release their weapons through a process called Resurrección. This returns their original Hollow powers and physical traits to their humanoid bodies, granting them immense strength and specialized techniques.

Her signature technique, Cero Doble, is one of the most formidable defensive and offensive abilities in the series. It showcases her unique capacity to absorb an incoming energy attack and fire it back with added force. This makes her an incredibly dangerous opponent for long-range fighters who rely heavily on energy blasts.

<Section icon={Map} title="Major Battles & Story Arcs">

Nelliel's narrative journey is closely intertwined with Ichigo's invasion of Hueco Mundo. After wandering the desert with her comedic companions, Pesche and Dondochakka, she encounters Ichigo's group and guides them through Las Noches. Her adult form is triggered when she witnesses Ichigo being brutally beaten by her old nemesis, Nnoitra Gilga. This emotional catalyst restores her memories and adult physique, allowing her to dominate the battle and showcase the terrifying gap in their power levels.

In the Thousand-Year Blood War arc, Nelliel returns to seek Ichigo's assistance after the Quincy Wandenreich empire invades Hueco Mundo and imprisons the de facto ruler, Tier Harribel. Armed with a special spiritual stabilization armband developed by Kisuke Urahara, Nelliel can now transition between her child and adult forms without losing her spiritual pressure, making her a vital ally in the final war against Yhwach.
