<Section icon={Target} title="Gameplay Mechanics and Chain Attack System">

Combat in Bleach Mirrors High is a real-time card-based action system, drawing comparisons to popular high-speed mobile fighters. However, it introduces unique mechanics like Chain Attacks and Switch Skills. Players build three-person teams and use attack cards to unleash physical combos and spiritual abilities.

When you initiate a Chain Attack, you switch characters mid-combo. Normally, switching characters incurs a standard 10-second cooldown. However, executing a successful Chain Attack reduces this cooldown to just 7 seconds, allowing for faster rotations and high-intensity pressure.

| Mechanic | Description | Cooldown Impact | Strategic Value |
| Basic Combo | Standard physical slashes | None | Builds spiritual level |
| Art Card Skill | Specialized character ability | Varied | Deals burst damage |
| Standard Switch | Manual swap to bench character | 10 Seconds | Emergency escape |
| Chain Attack | Switch during active combo | 7 Seconds | Maintains offensive pressure |
