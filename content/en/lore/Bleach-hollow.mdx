<Section icon={Target} title="The Origins of the Bleach hollow">

Understanding the **Bleach hollow** system begins with the transition of human souls, known as Pluses. When a soul lingers in the human world due to regret, obsession, or a severed Chain of Fate, it begins a slow process of corruption. This corrosion typically starts at the chest where the chain was once attached. Once the chain completely disappears, a hole opens in the chest—symbolizing the loss of the heart—and a white mask forms to protect the now-empty soul.

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Evolution Ladder: Menos Grande Classes">

When the void within a **Bleach hollow** becomes so immense that human souls no longer satisfy its hunger, it begins to cannibalize its own kind. This leads to the formation of a Menos Grande—a fusion of hundreds of Hollows into a single, massive entity. This evolutionary path is divided into three distinct tiers, each representing a massive jump in spiritual pressure (Reiatsu).

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Gillian (Lowest)">
    - **Massive Size**: Giant, slow-moving creatures.
    - **Low Intellect**: Most are mindless pawns.
    - **Uniformity**: Almost all share the same mask.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Adjuchas (Middle)">
    - **Smaller Size**: Human-sized or animal-like.
    - **High Intellect**: Capable of leading Gillians.
    - **Combat Power**: Can challenge Gotei 13 Captains.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vasto Lorde (Highest)">
    - **Humanoid**: Extremely rare and small.
    - **Elite Power**: Stronger than average Captains.
    - **Limited Numbers**: Only a handful exist in Hueco Mundo.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The evolution from Gillian to Adjuchas requires a "dominant personality" to take control of the collective mass. To maintain the Adjuchas form, the Hollow must continue devouring others. If an Adjuchas loses even a small piece of its body to another predator, its evolution permanently stops, and it risks regressing back into a mindless Gillian forever.

| Menos Class | Rarity | Primary Diet | Intelligence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gillian** | Common | Other standard Hollows | Very Low |
| **Adjuchas** | Rare | Other Menos Grande | High |
| **Vasto Lorde** | Extremely Rare | High-density Reiatsu | Genius/Human |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Arrancar: Breaking the Mask">

The ultimate state for a **Bleach hollow** is the Arrancar. By removing their mask, these beings gain Shinigami-like powers, including their own version of a sword called a Zanpakuto. Unlike Shinigami, whose swords release to change the blade, an Arrancar's Resurrección returns their original Hollow powers to their humanoid body.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Mask Removal">
    The Hollow shatters its bone mask, either naturally or through the influence of the Hogyoku.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Power Sealing">
    The core essence and original form of the Hollow are sealed into a sword-like weapon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Humanoid Manifestation">
    The creature takes on a mostly human appearance, retaining only a small fragment of its mask and a Hollow hole.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resurrección">
    By releasing the sword, the Arrancar regains its full Hollow strength and unique abilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Feature | Standard Hollow | Arrancar |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Weapon** | Claws/Teeth/Abilities | Zanpakuto (Resurrección) |
| **Appearance** | Monstrous | Humanoid |
| **Soul Type** | Corrupted | Hybrid (Shinigami-Hollow) |
| **Intelligence** | Variable | High/Human |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Abilities and Racial Traits">

Every **Bleach hollow** possesses a set of innate abilities that scale with their evolution. While a basic Hollow might only have "Acidic Touch," a Menos Grande or Arrancar can manipulate spiritual energy into devastating attacks.

**Key Hollow Traits:**
- **Cero**: A high-density blast of spiritual energy. The color and power vary by individual.
- **Bala**: A faster but weaker version of Cero, hardening spiritual pressure like a bullet.
- **Garganta**: The ability to tear open space to travel between Hueco Mundo and the World of the Living.
- **Pesquisa**: A sensory technique used to gauge the strength and location of nearby spiritual pressures.
- **Hierro**: The hardened skin of an Arrancar that acts as natural armor against blades.

| Ability | Description | Usage Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cero** | Destructive energy beam | Menos and above |
| **High-Speed Regeneration** | Healing lost limbs instantly | Most Hollows (lost by some Arrancars) |
| **Acidic Touch** | Corroding flesh upon contact | Basic Hollows |
| **Sonido** | High-speed movement technique | Arrancar exclusive |

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Note">
Many Arrancars sacrificed their High-Speed Regeneration in exchange for greater offensive power. Ulquiorra Cifer was a notable exception, maintaining elite-level healing even in his Resurrección.
</Callout>
