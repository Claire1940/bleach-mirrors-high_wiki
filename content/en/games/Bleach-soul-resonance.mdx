**Build Deep Dive:**
- **Mayuri:** Focus entirely on the bond that increases team Crit Damage. His personal damage is secondary to the utility he provides to Ichigo.
- **Kisuke:** Prioritize Energy Recharge. You want his support skills to be available the moment you swap him in, allowing for a 1-2 second field duration before returning to Ichigo.

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Pitfall">

<Section icon={Target} title="The 2-Minute Rotation Guide">

To achieve high-tier results in **Bleach Soul Resonance**, you must master the rhythm of the swap. The "Double Support" rotation is designed to keep Ichigo in his empowered state for as long as possible while ensuring all external buffs are active.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Buff Setup">
    Start with Ichigo to build initial meter, then immediately swap to Mayuri to trigger the team-wide Crit Damage buff. Use Mayuri's skill and swap out.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Energy Battery Phase">
    Bring in Kisuke to deploy his support field. This ensures Ichigo will have his Ultimate ready for the first major burn window.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Demon Time Activation">
    Swap back to Ichigo and activate his empowered state. Focus on high-damage skills while the Mayuri buff is still active (usually a 10-15 second window).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Refresh and Repeat">
    Once the buffs expire, perform a quick "circle swap" (Mayuri -> Kisuke -> Ichigo) to refresh all timers. Ensure you are quick-swapping on skill hits to maintain momentum.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Rotation Phase | Duration | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Buff Window** | 5-8 Seconds | Apply all off-field debuffs and buffs |
| **Burst Window** | 15-20 Seconds | Execute Ichigo's full skill rotation |
| **Cooldown Phase** | 5 Seconds | Refresh Energy and check buff timers |

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Tip">
Watch the top-right damage indicator. If your DPS drops below 3M, it usually means your Mayuri buff has expired. Refresh it immediately, even if Ichigo still has skills available.
</Callout>

</Section>
