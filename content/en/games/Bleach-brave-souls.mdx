### Attack Mechanics

- **Normal Attack String (NAD)**: Ichigo swings his baseball bat, sending energy balls flying at opponents. The final hit features Kon spinning rapidly after a powerful swing, dealing solid knockback.
- **Strong Attack 1 (SA1)**: A rapid, straight-line beam attack. It executes almost instantly, making it perfect for initiating combat or interrupting incoming enemy attacks.
- **Strong Attack 2 (SA2)**: A highly coveted tracking vortex. This attack summons a cyclone in front of Ichigo that actively hunts down and groups enemies together. This is arguably the best utility attack in the game.
- **Strong Attack 3 (SA3)**: A classic full-screen clearing move. It deals massive burst damage and guarantees wide-area coverage, making it ideal for clearing newly spawned waves.
- **Special Move (Soul Bomb)**: A comical yet devastating cinematic sequence of Ichigo and Kon training. It hits the entire screen, inflicts both Weakening and Slow, and resets critical cooldowns if built correctly.

The true test of any character in Bleach Brave Souls is their performance in Guild Quests. Dream Team Ichigo excels as a primary lead, especially during phases featuring Technique-attribute enemies where he enjoys a significant attribute advantage.

### Perfecting the Status Ailment Combo

Ichigo's ability to apply both **Weakening** and **Slow** simultaneously is his greatest asset. While standard Weakening only lasts a brief period, the Slow ailment persists much longer. By utilizing specialized builds, you can exploit these windows of vulnerability to melt boss health bars in seconds.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate with the Tracking Vortex">
    Cast Strong Attack 2 immediately as the boss spawns to group side mobs and lock the boss in place.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply Status Ailments">
    Use Strong Attack 1 or 3 to trigger the Weakening and Slow effects. The multi-hit nature of Frenzy +3 guarantees a high proc rate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Chain Your Strong Attacks">
    While the 50% Weakening damage bonus is active, quickly chain your remaining skills to maximize burst damage before the effect wears off.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Let Support Units DPS">
    Your active status ailments allow side damage dealers to inflict up to 90% more damage through specialized link setups.
  </Step>
</Steps>
